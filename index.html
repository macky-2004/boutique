Our Story

A Legacy Woven in Gold

For over a decade, AVIKA has been the destination for discerning women who seek more than just clothing — they seek artistry. Every ensemble in our atelier is a dialogue between age-old Indian craftsmanship and contemporary design sensibility.

Our master artisans work with the finest silks, hand-embroidered details, and heritage techniques passed down through generations, ensuring each piece is as unique as the woman who wears it.

— Meera Rathore, Founder
