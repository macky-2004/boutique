Since 2012

A Passion for the Exquisite

AVIKA was born from a singular vision — to create a space where the splendour of Indian textile heritage meets the precision of modern haute couture. Founded by Meera Rathore in the vibrant heart of Jaipur, our atelier has spent over a decade perfecting the art of wearable poetry.

Every piece that leaves our studio is the result of hundreds of hours of meticulous handwork. Our master artisans — many of whom represent the sixth or seventh generation of their craft families — work with endangered textiles, antique zardozi techniques, and hand-spun silks sourced from across the subcontinent.

From the bustling bazaars of Varanasi to the serene block-printing villages of Bagru, we travel India to bring you the very finest of what our country's unparalleled textile traditions have to offer. Each garment carries not just a label, but a legacy.

Our Ethos

What We Stand For

Three pillars guide everything we create.

♡

Craftsmanship

Every stitch is a testament to generations of mastery. We work exclusively with master artisans who practise centuries-old techniques, ensuring each piece is a work of art.

★

Authenticity

We source directly from heritage clusters across India — from the silks of Kanchipuram to the embroideries of Lucknow — preserving authenticity in every thread.

♦

Sustainability

Slow fashion is our commitment. We create limited-edition pieces using handloom fabrics, natural dyes, and zero-waste cutting techniques, honouring both people and planet.
